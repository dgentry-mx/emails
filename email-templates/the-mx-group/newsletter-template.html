June 12, 2024
All,

I hope this finds you well. In our last newsletter I mentioned that I could feel the momentum within the agency building. That our efforts to simplify our structure and focus our energy were working. And I am happy to say that April was our best month to date.

Since then, we’ve started new work with Fibertech focused on significant aspects of their growth strategy. We’ve expanded our work with Grundfos and First Citizens Bank, who understand our efforts are drivers of their growth. And we’ve seen our pipeline grow, in part from client referrals that result from a great experience with MX.

Keeping the momentum going over the summer months will require a lot of coordination and communication: We must be intentional in our planning to account for vacations within the agency and among our clients. We need to be proactive to keep our teams engaged on client priorities while we onboard new clients. And we have to work together to take on the challenges our clients are faced with and help them generate growth. Maintaining this momentum is important for our clients as they need to generate results — and it’s equally important for MX as we navigate a complicated market.

When we do this right, it will feel natural for our clients and for you. I think one of our clients put it best by saying, “It isn’t easy to find an agency that brings rich ideas to life, that also fits into your company culture effortlessly, but with MX that’s certainly been the case. MX is truly an extension of our internal team, helping us foster synergies that are usually aspirational at most for client-agency relationships.”

Let’s keep the momentum going.

Talk soon,
Tony
 
 
Milestones
Happy 7th Anniversary, Kelly!
Believe it or not, Friday, June 7, marks Kelly Olson’s 20th anniversary as an MX’er. It’s almost impossible to quantify Kelly’s contributions to the company over two decades — as an account coordinator and manager in our old building across the street, to being an accomplished account director overseeing Tempur Sealy through its biggest transitions as a hospitality business, to leading our Client Services department through change in the industry, to blazing a new trail as the head of strategy — a new department she helped create in our agency. Kelly is applauded and appreciated by clients and colleagues who have had the pleasure of working with her. Her calm presence, curiosity and extensive experience in B2B marketing are why we are all lucky to have her on our team. Congratulations, Kelly, and thank you for 20 years of kicking ass!
